Price Variation Web Scraping

A powerful web scraping tool designed to collect and analyze supermarket product data from DIA.

Key Features:
- Price Variation Analysis: Track price changes over time to uncover market trends and pricing strategies.
- Data Visualization: Create interactive graphs and dashboards to explore pricing, discounts, and best-selling categories.
- Price Comparison: Compare prices across supermarkets to find the best deals.
- Inventory & Assortment Optimization: Identify products with frequent price fluctuations for smarter inventory and marketing strategies.
- Impact of Discount Campaigns: Analyze how promotions affect product prices and demand.

Use Cases:
- Inflation and economic trend monitoring.
- Price alerts and anomaly detection.
- Consumer behavior research and predictive modeling.
- Benchmarking for suppliers and smart shopping apps.

Technologies Used:
- Selenium, Requests, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, NumPy.
