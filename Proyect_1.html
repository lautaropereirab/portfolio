Web Scraper

A powerful web scraping tool designed to collect and analyze supermarket product data from DIA.

Key Features:

• Price Variation Analysis
• Data Visualization of discounts and categories
• Price Comparison across supermarkets
• Inventory & Assortment Optimization
• Impact of Discount Campaigns analysis

Use Cases:

• Inflation and economic trend monitoring
• Price alerts and anomaly detection
• Consumer behavior research
• Benchmarking for suppliers

Technologies Used:
Selenium, Requests, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, NumPy
