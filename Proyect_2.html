Data Visualization

Visualization of a retail sales dataset to uncover patterns and generate actionable insights through interactive and static charts.

Key Features:

• Interactive charts for data exploration
• Static/custom visuals for specific questions
• Views by products, revenue, categories, and more

Technologies Used:
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Plotly Express, Matplotlib, (Seaborn para exploración)

Use Cases:
• Identify sales trends & best-selling products
• Analyze customer behavior (edad, frecuencia, ticket)
• Support inventory & marketing decisions
